Questions owners ask

Before you start.

Why is my coffee shop busy but not profitable?

A full café isn't the same as a profitable one. Strong sales can hide a rising labor ratio, a shrinking customer count behind a higher average ticket, or afternoon hours that barely cover the staff running them. Second Manager reads your POS data every week and points to exactly these gaps between "looks busy" and "actually making money" — so you catch them while they're still small.

What is Second Manager?

It's a weekly insights tool built exclusively for independent specialty coffee shops. You upload your Square or Toast sales export, and it surfaces the questions your numbers are quietly raising — like a second manager who reads your reports every Monday morning. It doesn't hand you a verdict or a to-do list. It makes sure you're asking the right questions about your own shop.

Which POS systems does it work with?

Square and Toast today. You don't connect an account or set up an integration — you just download your weekly sales reports as a CSV and drop them in. Using Clover, Lightspeed, or a spreadsheet? Join the waitlist below and we'll reach out when your POS is supported.

What reports do I need to upload?

Two to three files from Square or Toast — your Hourly Sales report, your Item Sales report, and optionally your Timecard report if you want labor insights. No API keys, no setup. We show you exactly where to find each file inside your POS.

How much time does it take each week?

About five minutes. Download your reports, upload them, and read three questions worth sitting with. Your weekly digest also lands in your inbox every Monday, so you get the one thing worth watching without even logging in.

Is there a limit on how many analyses I can run?

During your 7-day free trial you get 2 analyses to try things out. Once you're on a paid plan, that becomes 10 analyses per billing cycle — one analysis per upload, which is comfortably more than a weekly rhythm needs, with a few extra to re-run or experiment. Your count resets at the start of each billing cycle, and you can always see how many you have left right inside the app.

Is my sales data private?

Yes. Any customer names, emails, or phone numbers in your export are stripped out before your data is ever analyzed — the analysis only ever sees anonymized patterns, never your customers' personal details.

Where do the benchmarks come from?

They're drawn from recognized coffee and restaurant industry sources — including the Specialty Coffee Association (SCA), National Coffee Association (NCA), IBISWorld, the National Restaurant Association (NRA), and coffee data from Square and Toast — then adjusted to your shop's specific profile: your city tier, shop maturity, staff size, and seating type. That way the comparison fits a shop like yours, not a generic average. And your own uploaded data always stays private — it's never shared, and never becomes part of these benchmarks.

What does it cost, and can I cancel?

Seven days free, then $49/month at the early-bird rate. No contracts, no minimums — cancel any time from your account settings.
